About Me

I began my journey in Mechanical Engineering, but my curiosity for technology led me toward software development. After completing my graduation, I moved to Hyderabad and joined J-Spiders to learn Java Full Stack Development, where I built my foundation in coding and web technologies.

During training, I developed small web projects using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while also practicing Java programming and aptitude to strengthen my problem-solving skills. After several interviews, I received my first offer from Suneratech, marking the start of my software engineering career.

At Suneratech, I worked on the Greenworks Tools project, gaining hands-on experience in EDI, XML, and MuleSoft integration. Later, I contributed to Waystar, focusing on healthcare EDI and Salesforce-related integrations, which helped me explore both B2B and healthcare domains.

Currently, I’m part of Accenture, working on the WSFG (ALIP) project in the insurance domain. Here, I specialize in Java, Spring Integration, SQL, Azure, and API testing tools, continuously improving my skills and delivering scalable backend solutions.
